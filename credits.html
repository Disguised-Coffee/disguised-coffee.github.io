A quick few words

Although it may sound cliche and over said at points of time, I thank my teachers and parents for pushing, motivating, and teaching me. They are the ones who sacrifice themselves and their desires to put up with a test of patience and mental will. Without them, I wouldn't have been able to be where I am today nor would I have ever gotten into computer science. Without them, I wouldn't have known of the multiple paths I could take and the ones I should avoid. Without them, I wouldn't have even known what 9 + 10 is or my left from my right.
My life would have been much harder without any of them.

So then, I assume that the least I can say is thanks ma, thanks pa.

Thank you to the many teachers I've had and the teachers I will have.

- Disguised_Coffee, 7/21/22
